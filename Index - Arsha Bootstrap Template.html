Electricity Consumption Analysis

Our Work Process

The project followed a systematic 4-step process. First, we collected electricity consumption data from official government portals. Second, we cleaned and prepared the data for analysis. Third, we used Tableau to create interactive visualizations and dashboards. Finally, we published the complete analysis as a Tableau Story and embedded it into this website.

About Electricity

This project analyzes electricity consumption across Indian states using Tableau. We visualize usage patterns, seasonal trends, and year-on-year growth to provide clear insights. The interactive dashboards help understand which states consume the most power and when demand peaks. Our goal is to make energy data simple and accessible for better planning and decision-making.

  • State-wise electricity usage visualization
  • Track seasonal and yearly consumption trends
  • Interactive Tableau dashboards and story
